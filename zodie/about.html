About Zodie

Zodie is a dating website that focuses on using zodiac signs to find appropriate matches for its users. Zodie is a startup company formed in 2017 by a small group of astrology enthusiasts from Minneapolis that were purchased by and given funding from their now parent company HBS Inc.; on whose board and executive team can be found a disproportionate number of astrology enthusiasts relative to your average holding company. Instead of finding matches based off an impersonal algorithm or relying specifically on user photographs, Zodie bases its matches on the immemorial and infallible system of the stars themselves.
